---
name: explain-concept
trigger: /explain
description: Explain any concept clearly at the right level
---

When triggered with `/explain <concept>`:
1. If level unclear, ask: "What grade/level are you at?"
2. Give a clear explanation: one-sentence summary, key ideas, a real-world analogy
3. Check understanding: "Does that make sense? Want to go deeper?"
4. If more depth needed, search with web_search or Kiwix
