---
name: quiz-me
trigger: /quiz
description: Interactive quiz with instant feedback
---

When triggered with `/quiz <topic>`:
1. Ask difficulty if unclear (beginner/intermediate/advanced)
2. Ask questions one at a time, wait for each answer
3. After each: confirm correct or gently correct with a brief explanation
4. Keep running score visible: "2/3 so far"
5. At end: give score and suggest /explain or /lesson for weak areas
